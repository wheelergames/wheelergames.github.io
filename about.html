--- layout: page title: About Wheeler Games short_description: What we aim to do, and how you can keep in contact with us. ---

Wheeler Games is a solo dev bringing adaptations of board games to the digital world, via mobile apps and in the future PC and console versions too.

Starting out in 2017, I have so far bought two games to market on Android and iOS, with a third game currently underway, which will hopefully also come to PC and possibly even Nintendo Switch as well.

For more information, follow me on Twitter, sign up to the monthly email and join in all the discussion on the Discord Channel.
